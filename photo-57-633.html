Home Application

DIPIPM are widely used to control compressor motors, fan motors, and drum motors in air conditioners, refrigerators, and washing machines, and other small motor application.

Features of Electric power modules for home appliances
Wide lineup (for inverter, for PFC, built-in bootstrap diode type and built-in rectifier diode type)
Small size and high performance
Low loss and low thermal impedance
High quality and reliability
